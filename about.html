Our Story

FitHome Pro was born from a simple belief: Everyone deserves access to quality fitness guidance, regardless of their budget, location, or experience level.

Founded in 2020 by a team of certified fitness professionals and nutrition experts, we recognized a critical gap in the fitness industry. While expensive gym memberships and personal training sessions remained out of reach for millions, the internet was flooded with unverified workout advice and dangerous fitness trends.

The Problem We Saw

People were spending hundreds of dollars on gym memberships they rarely used, following unqualified "fitness influencers" promoting harmful quick-fixes, and feeling discouraged by their lack of progress. The fitness industry had become inaccessible, confusing, and often unsafe for the average person.

Our Solution

We created FitHome Pro as a 100% free, science-backed fitness platform that brings professional-quality workout programs, nutrition guidance, and expert advice directly to your home. No expensive equipment. No gym membership. No confusion.

Every workout routine is designed by certified personal trainers. Every nutrition guide is reviewed by registered dietitians. Every piece of advice is backed by peer-reviewed research.

The Impact

Today, FitHome Pro serves over 500,000 active members across 150+ countries. Our community has collectively lost over 2 million pounds, gained countless muscle, and more importantly, developed sustainable healthy habits that last a lifetime.

But we're not done yet. Our mission continues: to make professional fitness guidance accessible to everyone, everywhere, completely free.
